Lasagna

Description

Treat yourself to the comforting flavours of a classic lasagna dish. Each bite is a symphony of layers, from delicate pasta sheets to robust meat sauce, velvety bechemel, and a blanket of gooey melted cheese. Whether it's a cosy family dinner or a special gathering with friends, lasagna is a timeless crowd-pleaser that never fails to satisfy.
