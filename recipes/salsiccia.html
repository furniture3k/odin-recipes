Salsiccia Recipe

Home

Description

This rigatoni with Italian sausage recipe (Pasta con Salsiccia) is made with simple ingredients yet is packed with flavour. Great for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 300g Italian sausage (salsiccia)
    • 2 yellow onions, chopped
  • 2 garilic clives, minced
  • 100ml dry white wine
  • 700g glass pureed tomatoes
  • 1.4 tsp smoked paprika
    • 1 sprig fresh rosemary Sea salt and pepper 2 tbsp creme fraiche 500g dried rigatoni grated parmesean cheese to serve

Steps

  1. In a large deep skillet (or wok) heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until soft and translucent (8-10 minutes). Add the sausage, increase the heat slightly, and cook for 5 minutes, using a wooden spoon to break up the meat.
  2. Add the wine, and cook for 3-4 minutes, until reduced and almost completely evaporated. Then add the tomato passata, paprika and rosemary; season with salt and pepper.
  3. Bring to a simmer, then reduce the heat to medium-low and continue cooking for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in crème fraîche.
  4. Meanwhile, in a large pot of boiling, salted water, cook the rigatoni, according to package instructions, until al dente. Drain the pasta and toss with the sauce. Serve with grated parmesan.
